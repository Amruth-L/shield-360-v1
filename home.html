Introduction to Shield 360

Shield 360 is a child safety and awareness platform created to protect children in both the digital and physical world. It aims to educate children, parents, and educators about the risks associated with online activities, cyberbullying, inappropriate content, and real-world dangers.

Our Mission

To empower children, parents, and educators with knowledge, resources, and tools to stay safe in the digital and physical world. We strive to build a community where every child can learn and grow without fear or risk.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Shield 360 is to create awareness about child protection laws, online safety measures, and to provide guidance on how to respond to threats effectively, ensuring a secure environment for all children.
