Problem

Children face sexual abuse and lack awareness of the POCSO Act. Shield 360 aims to prevent abuse and ensure safety through awareness and reporting.

Goal

Build a safe ecosystem with fun learning, easy reporting, and real-time protection for kids, parents, and teachers.

POCSO

Protects children from sexual offenses, ensures legal support, and empowers kids to speak up safely.

Cyber Safety

Keeps children and data safe online, prevents threats, and promotes secure digital habits.
